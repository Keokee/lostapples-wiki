The Lost Apples Wiki

This wiki is being produced as an adjunct to publication of the newly released book
Lost Apples: The search for rare and heritage apples in the Pacific Northwest
by David Benscoter and Linda Hackbarth.

The wiki will be developed as a place for apple hunters, researchers and enthusiasts to contribute, trade and archive information for the potential recovery of historic apple cultivars in the region.

The wiki will launch in October 2024.
